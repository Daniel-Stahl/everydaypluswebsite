Clip-Ins

Clip-In Hair Extensions

The Everyday Plus collection offers Clip In extensions. Clip-Ins are great for those who wish to add length and or fullness to their own hair. This 5 piece set is available in 18 inches and is sold in an assortment of colors.

Wavy

Weft Hair Extensions

Everyday Plus collection offers a Wavy weft that is also 100% Human Remy hair that is steamed permed. Wavy is the loosest of the collection and can vary between a wave and a natural body.

Halo

Halo Hair Extensions

For those seeking a quick and easy one step method to achieve fullness and or add a little length to their natural hair Everyday Plus offers the Halo. A Halo consists of machine weft sewn onto a lace base with a thin and clear fish wire that allows the piece to adhere to the head. The fish wire is undetectable once styled. No clips needed, just place on head and style per usual.

Curly

Weft Hair Extensions

Everyday Plus collection offers a Curly weft for those who want to achieve a soft curl with body. Curly hair is 100% Human Remy hair that is steamed permed on 1/2 inch rods to give a deeper curl pattern. Steam perming the hair allows for preserving the quality of the hair.

Kinky

Weft Hair Extensions

Kinky Curly was designed for the person whom desires a more Afrocentric/Textured Curl. Kinky Curly is 100% Human Remy hair that is steamed permed on 1/8 inch rods. Steam perming the hair allows a uniformed curl, which is a much desired look. Heat is not recommended due to loss of curl over time.
